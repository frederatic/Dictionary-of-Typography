Dictionary of Typography

beautiful words typed in fantastic fonts

Epiphany

a sudden realization

I had a epihany that I've never actually seen my real face, only pictures and reflections.

Surreal

so strange as to appear imaginary

I am not a handsome man, so it felt surreal when the most beautiful woman at the party asked me to dance.

Fluffy

covered with fluff

It's so fluffy, i'm gonna die!

Unicorn

a majestic, mythical horse with a horn

Look at that fluffy unicorn!

Phenomenal

very remarkable; extraordinary

Sending a car into space is phenomenal.

Succulent

tender, juicy, and tasty

That chicken was so succulent.

Nemesis

an unconquerable archenemy

Lex Luthor is Superman's nemesis.

Explosion

a sudden outburst of something

Cool guys don't look at explosions.

Eloquence

fluent or persuasive speaking or writing

His eloquence had a great hold upon the masses.

Eternity

infinite or unending time

After what felt like an eternity, I finally finished.

Smooth

completing a task with such grace and fluidity that it looks easy

Lebron's jumps are always smooth.

Ineffable

too great to be expressed in words

The beauty of life is ineffable.

Quintessential

representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality; classic

Fred is the quintessential smart guy.

Paradigm

a typical example or pattern of something; a model

The cognitive revolution was the greatest paradigm shift in psychology.
